Mantis Shrimp

The mantis shrimp is a marine crustacean known for its powerful claws.

Habitat

The mantis shrimp is typically found in shallow, tropical, and subtropical waters.

Diet

They are carnivorous and known for their incredible speed and strength when capturing prey.

Mantis Shrimp Characteristics
Feature Description
Eyes Highly complex with trinocular vision
Claws Can strike with the speed of a bullet
Size Up to 18 cm (7 in)
